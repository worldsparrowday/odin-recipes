Shrimp pasta

Quick and easy shrimp pasta made with few ingredients

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • butter
  • garlic
  • shrimp
  • cream
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta al dente
  2. Butter in pan, put in chopped up garlic
  3. Add shrimp after garlic is golden
  4. Add cream and whatever else
  5. Put pasta in pan
  6. Season, let cook
